The All New Honda CB190R

The Honda CB190R is a naked streetfighter motorcycle that is sold by Honda in selected countries. It comes with a muscular and aggressive looking design. It is powered by a single-cylinder 4-stroke engine that is mated to a 5-speed gearbox.

CB190R In A Nutshell
Air Cooled 184cc, Single-Cylinder, SOHC, 4-Valves. Compression ratio of 9.5:1 with a PGM-Fi Fuel Injection.
5-Speed transmission on a Wet-Multi Plate clutch. 0-60 km/h in 4.5s and 0-100 km/h in 16s.
Hits 16.9 HP with 16 Nm of torque at 7,000 rpm. Able to achieve 120 km/h with a fuel consumption of 42 km/l.
Inverted Telescopic forks with 17 inch wheel size. Single Piston Calliper Brake Disc, non-ABS. Kerb weight of 142kg with 12l of fuel and 1l of engine oil.
Its sports bike stance, with racy Italian styling cues, makes it an instant hit with younger riders. If you want a great-looking, well-equipped bike that is relatively affordable and which promises low running cost, you can do far worse than this China-made bike.
-Torque SG, 2016
